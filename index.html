Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a University Student studying at University College Utrecht, part of Utrecht University, in the Netherlands.

My major is Earth & Environment combined with Biology. For a summer lab course, I took a course in Geographical Information Systems, taught by Professor Britta Ricker.

This website is a showcase of the maps and apps that I made during the course, as well as a contact page to get into contact with me. Enjoy!



About

I am a first-year student, interested in environmental sciences. I am particularly interested in nature conservation and ecosystem management. In the modern world, ecosystems are under immense anthropogenic pressure. Ecosystems need help to survive healthily, and I want to help. That’s why I’m hoping to get into research and implementation of environmental sciences.
